Why Choosing Us!

Few Reasons Why People Choosing Us!

Agomoni, established in 2023, is the first non-profit Indian organization in the Netherlands to have all women office bearers working hand in hand with their male counterparts as a foundation.

The name "Agomoni" is derived from the Bengali language and translates to "ushering." For the group of like-minded enthusiasts involved, Agomoni serves as a means of fostering friendly camaraderie and a sense of togetherness. Through Agomoni, they aim not only to create a home away from home but also to pass down the rich cultural traditions and heritage of India to future generations who are growing up outside their motherland. Agomoni extends a warm welcome to all who wish to join their platform for exchanging views and cultural amalgamation. They celebrate togetherness and strive to foster relationships with integrity, sustainability, and trust.

Our mission is to promote Indian cultural legacies through socio-cultural events organized throughout the year. We endeavour to bring synergy between Indian culture and the Dutch and greater European societies to encourage a healthy learning environment.

Our vision is to cultivate a sense of unity while having fun and instill cultural richness in future generations. Through enjoyment and togetherness, we aim to spread our diverse culture among all communities for learning and sharing.

As we embark on our journey of embracing India's cultural traditions, we warmly welcome everyone with open arms.
